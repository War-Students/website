Why people should care about Ukraine and info about the project

The War&Studetns Project was created to show war from the the students' point of view. On this website you can read stories from Ukrainian students on and how how the war affected our studies, daily habits, mental and physical conditions. Also, you feel free to find more information on hot to support Ukraine at this difficult time. If you are a student from Ukraine please find more opportunities for studies here. You are also welcome to share your own story. The authors of the project are Ukrainian writers, illustrators, programmers, and scientists.
